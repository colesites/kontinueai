## Features

### 🔁 Resume Any Conversation
Import your existing chats from any major AI platform. Every prompt, document, and thread lives in one unified workspace that remembers your context perfectly.

### ⚡ Instant Model Switching
Switch between ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Grok, Llama, Mistral, Perplexity, Qwen, and more with a single click — without losing your context.

### 💸 One Plan, All Models
Stop paying for multiple $20/month subscriptions. Access the highest tiers of every major AI model under one transparent price.

### 🎙️ Voice Without Borders
Send audio in any language and get precise transcriptions that understand nuance and emotion — perfect for dictating notes or recording conversations.

### 🖼️ Image Generation
Turn ideas into high-fidelity visuals instantly. From urban photography to character portraits, professional-grade image generation is built right in.

### 🎬 Video Generation *(Pro)*
Generate cinematic, coherent video content powered by next-gen AI. Professional motion at the speed of thought.

### 🧠 Emotional Intelligence
Models that go beyond raw processing — perceiving tone, intent, and sentiment to deliver responses that feel human and truly collaborative.
